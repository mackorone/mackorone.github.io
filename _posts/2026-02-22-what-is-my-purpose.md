---
title: "What is My Purpose?"
---

I've been thinking a lot about my value as a human in the age of AI. What do I
have to offer the world when AI can do everything better than I can?

My conclusion for now is that *being human* is the only value I will have. Not
because I think AI won't be able to do human-like tasks; even things that seem
inherently human, like understanding other humans, will be conquered by AI.

But because humans will want to see themselves in other people. AI can teach
people *how* something is done. But teachers, coaches, and role models---other
humans---will always be necessary to show people *that* it can be done---that
they, too, can do it if they try hard enough.

"You have to see it to be it." --- Billie Jean King
